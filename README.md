# Anti-Bullying App – Together

## Overview
Together is a mobile application designed to support students aged 12 to 22 who experience bullying. The app provides a safe space where users can learn about bullying, recognize its signs, gain confidence in dealing with bullies, and seek help when needed.

Our goal is to raise awareness about bullying and provide practical guidance and support for those affected by it.

## Features

### 1. Educational Resources
- Information about bullying and its different forms.
- Guidance on understanding bullying and its effects.

### 2. Recognizing the Signs
- Helps users identify signs that may indicate they are being bullied.
- Provides examples of common bullying behaviors.

### 3. Building Confidence
- Tips and strategies for facing bullies safely.
- Advice on improving self-confidence and resilience.

### 4. Seeking Help
- Guidance on when and how to ask for help.
- Encourages users to reach out to trusted adults, teachers, counselors, or support services.

### 5. Emergency Contact Numbers
- Quick access to important emergency and support contacts.
- Helps users find assistance when immediate help is needed.

## Purpose
The app aims to:
- Raise awareness about bullying.
- Educate users on how to recognize bullying.
- Provide strategies for dealing with bullying situations.
- Encourage users to seek help and support when necessary.

## Target Audience
Students and young people between the ages of **12 and 22**.

## Project Goal
To create a supportive and informative platform that empowers young people to recognize, respond to, and overcome bullying in a safe and confident manner.
